We are excited to launch the new website for the **Tianming Yang Lab**, a research group 
focused on understanding the **neural mechanisms of decision making and cognition**.

Our work bridges **neuroscience and AI**. We study how neural circuits give rise to perception, 
learning, decision-making, complex problem-solving, and consciousness, and how these 
principles may help inform the next generation of intelligent systems.

On this website, you can explore:

- **Research** — Learn about our research directions.
- **News & Blog** — Follow lab updates, paper announcements, and research commentary.
- **Publications** — Browse selected papers related to decision making and cognition.
- **People** — Meet current lab members and alumni.
- **Contact** — Reach out to us or apply to join the lab.

We hope this website will serve as a place to share our research, connect with collaborators, 
and communicate new ideas at the intersection of brain science, cognition, and intelligent systems.

Welcome, and thank you for visiting!
