01 — Research

What we study

What happens in our brain when we are thinking? Our lab combines sophisticated behavioral experiments, electrophysiology in non-human primates, fMRI in humans, and computational modeling to understand how networks of neurons process information, accumulate evidence, evaluate options, compute choices, and solve complex problems.

Evidence Accumulation

How does the brain integrate noisy sensory and value information over time to form decisions? We have shown that evidence accumulation occurs in parietal and prefrontal cortex.

Value, Attention, & Decision Making

Value information is encoded in the prefrontal cortex. We investigate how attention and salience reshape the value signal during economic choice.
